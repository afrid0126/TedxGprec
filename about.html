About TED

TED (Technology, Entertainment, Design) is a non-profit media organization that shares free global talks under the slogan Ideas worth spreading." Covering almost from science to business to global issues, TED features thought-provoking short talks, demos, and performances that spark curiosity, ignite imagination, and start important conversations.

About TEDx

TEDx is a grassroots initiative modeled after TED, where local communities organize independent events to share powerful ideas These events are independently curated but adhere to TEDâ€™s spirit: ideas-driven, open-source, diverse, and community-centric. TEDx fosters innovation, leadership, and creativity showcasing voices that truly have the power to change the world.

About TEDxGPREC

TEDxGPREC is a student-led, officially licensed TEDx event at GPREC. It embodies our belief that ideas shape reality Under the theme "Unboxing the New Reality" we bring together trailblazing speakers and captivating performers to inspire and challenge perspectives. Blending the TED ethos with Indian culture and student creativity, TEDxGPREC creates a space where voices spark change and thought becomes action.
